What is Efilwol?

Efilwol is a browser-based tactical RPG and a spiritual successor to Lowlife, the beloved iOS game. If you spent hours building parties, grinding stages, and obsessing over elemental matchups in Lowlife — you're in the right place.

The name? Efilwol is Lowlife spelled backwards. A small nod to where it all started.

⚔️

Build Your Party

Assemble up to 5 scripts — melee fighters in the front row, ranged attackers in the back. Each script has unique stats, elemental typing, and a set of 6 actions drawn from its pool.

🔥

Elemental Combat

New to Efilwol — every action has an elemental type and every defender has a specialization. Fire melts ice. Lightning shreds water. Holy smites dark. Land the right matchup for a 2× damage bonus, or face a glancing blow. A layer of strategy the original Lowlife never had.

👁️

Efilwol the Healer

Efilwol is the mysterious healer at the heart of your party. While your scripts fight on the front and back lines, Efilwol keeps them alive — casting heals between battles and turning the tide when things get desperate. Guard them well.

Early Access

Efilwol is actively in development. New stages, scripts, actions, and mechanics are being added regularly. Create a free account to save your progress, earn XP, and help shape the game — feedback and suggestions are always welcome.
